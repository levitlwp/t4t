Treasures 4 teachers

means riches for education

Treasures 4 Teachers is a member based resource and supply store serving the educators of Arizona. This means that anyone who works closely with students can have access to a wide selection of materials, resources and training that will enhance and expand the learning process. T4T is a donation-based operation. Therefore, we are able to provide low cost and free school supplies to our members in Tucson, Arizona.

Went today for the first time and didn't want to leave!! Neither did my preschooler:) This place has everything a new/experienced teacher, Sunday school teacher, homeschool mom could want. I am just starting to homeschool and don't have any supplies- for $45 (including the one time membership fee of $35) I left with a trunk full of great supplies to help me. Adrian went above and beyond to help me find what I needed and gave me so many great suggestions of activities I could do- since she was a teacher herself. Fabric, scrapbook pads of paper, seasonal crafts, binders, file cabinets... They have it all! I only wish I found this sooner! I'll be back at least once a week:) thank you Treasures 4 Teachers!!
